# Connector Validation and Certification

Validation packs are written to:

`artifacts/connectors/validation/{connector_id}/`

Each pack contains:

- `manifest.json`
- `data_contract.json`
- `mapping_rules.json`
- `validation_report.json`
- `sample_output.json`

Use the validation pack to support connector verification, customer CSV discussion, and partner certification review. The local harness is not a claim of compliance or certification by itself.
