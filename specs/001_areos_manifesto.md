# 001 Areos Manifesto

Areos is a **system of assurance, not a system of record**.

- Do not sell monitoring; sell proof.
- Existing systems monitor signals; Areos links signals to validated state, product impact, and audit evidence.
- Utility event → area → batch/product/material → quality risk → evidence → decision.
- AI assists evidence generation; humans approve quality decisions.

## Why monitoring alone is not enough

Plants already run MES, QMS, BMS/EMS, SCADA, historians, CMMS, LIMS, ERP, and OEE systems. Monitoring does not automatically produce cross-system, audit-ready impact evidence. Areos focuses on that assurance gap.

## Why OT/IT/GxP convergence is the moat

Assurance requires consistent context across utility behavior, production state, maintenance history, and quality records. Areos converges those domains while preserving read-only-first OT safety.

## Why AWS-native services

Managed, production-grade building blocks (IoT Core, SiteWise, S3, EventBridge, Step Functions, Bedrock, IAM/KMS/Cognito) reduce architecture drift between MVP and enterprise deployment.

## Regulatory posture

This platform is designed to support validation workflows and human QA decisions. It does not claim autonomous release authority or regulator approval.
